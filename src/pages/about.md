# c0redumpsec

c0redumpsec is a place for documenting my cybersecurity learning journey.

This blog is where I collect practical notes, lab writeups, tooling experiments, research threads, and lessons learned while working through security topics. Some posts will be polished walkthroughs; others may be rough notes from the middle of a problem, a rabbit hole, or a failed approach that still taught me something useful.

The focus is hands-on learning: understanding how systems behave, breaking problems down, testing assumptions, and building knowledge through practice. Topics may include defensive security, labs, tooling, reverse engineering, vulnerability research, Linux, scripting, and general security concepts.

I write to make the learning process visible — not just the final answer, but the steps, mistakes, observations, and reasoning along the way.

> All content is intended for authorised testing, research, education, and defensive security purposes only.
